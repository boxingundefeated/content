### 3. What are the benefits of using a 1 inch thick yoga mat?

Some benefits of using a 1 inch thick yoga mat include:

### 4. How do I clean my 1 inch thick yoga mat?

To clean your 1 inch thick yoga mat, follow these steps:   

1. Mix equal parts water and mild detergent.

2. Apply the mixture gently onto the mat using a soft cloth or sponge.

3. Gently clean the entire surface of the mat, focusing on any dirty or sweaty areas.

4. Rinse the mat thoroughly with clean water, ensuring no soap residue remains.

5. Allow the mat to air dry completely before rolling it up and storing it away.
