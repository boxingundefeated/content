### How can I store and maintain my 15 lb kettlebell?

Proper storage and maintenance of your kettlebell are essential to keep it in good condition and ensure it lasts. After each use, clean the kettlebell with a damp cloth to remove any sweat or dirt. Store the kettlebell in a dry, well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight or extreme temperatures to prevent rust and damage. To prevent accidental injuries, ensure the kettlebell is not stored near sharp or heavy objects that can fall on it or obstruct your access to it during workouts. 


### Are there any safety precautions to consider when using a 15 lb kettlebell?

Safety is paramount when using any fitness equipment, including kettlebells. Before starting a workout, ensure you have a clear and unobstructed workout area. Start with lighter weights and gradually progress, focusing on perfecting your form before increasing the resistance. Additionally, always be aware of your surroundings and never leave the kettlebell unattended or within reach of children or pets.
