An Airdyne bike is unique in that it uses air resistance to provide workout intensity. The harder you pedal, the more resistance is generated. ### Do Airdyne bikes require maintenance?

Like any fitness equipment, Airdyne bikes require some level of maintenance to ensure optimal performance and longevity. Regular maintenance tasks include: 

* Keep the bike clean and free of dust and debris, especially around the air resistance system.

* Periodically inspect and tighten any loose screws or bolts to prevent mechanical issues.

* Ensure the chain and pedal system are properly lubricated to protect against wear and tear.
