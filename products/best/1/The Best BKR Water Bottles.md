### How do I clean my BKR water bottle?

Cleaning your BKR water bottle is easy. Simply remove the silicone sleeve and hand wash both the sleeve and bottle with warm, soapy water. You can also use a bottle brush to gently clean the interior of the bottle. Make sure to rinse thoroughly and air dry the parts before reassembling the bottle. 


### Do BKR water bottles leak?

BKR water bottles are designed to be leak-resistant when used properly. Ensure that the silicone sleeve is securely fitted onto the glass bottle, and don't overfill the bottle past the fill line. When stored upright in a bag or gym tote, BKR water bottles should not leak. However, it is essential to maintain and store your water bottle responsibly to ensure maximum leak protection. 


### How do I replace the silicone sleeve on my BKR water bottle?

To replace the silicone sleeve on your BKR water bottle, first, clean the bottle and sleeve thoroughly with warm, soapy water. Next, locate the two small notches on the bottom edge of the sleeve and align them with the corresponding notches on the glass bottle. Begin sliding the sleeve upwards, ensuring that the notches remain aligned. Once the sleeve is halfway up the bottle, check that it is seated correctly and proceed to slide it the rest of the way up the bottle. Gently tug on the bottom edge of the sleeve to ensure it is securely fastened.
