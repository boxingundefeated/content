### How do I clean my Bear Water Bottle?

To clean your Bear Water Bottle, follow these simple steps: - Stainless steel bottles: Rinse the bottle with warm, soapy water, then use a bottle brush to scrub the interior. Rinse thoroughly and let it air dry. Do not use dishwasher detergent, as it can damage the paint. - Glass bottles: Similarly, rinse with warm, soapy water and wash gently with a soft brush. Rinse thoroughly and air dry. - Silicone bottles: Wash with warm soapy water and a soft brush, making sure to clean all crevices. Rinse and air dry. Avoid using any harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as they can damage the material. 


###  Can I put Bear Water Bottles in the dishwasher?

While some Bear Water Bottles are dishwasher safe, it is generally not recommended, as prolonged exposure to high heat can damage the paint on stainless steel bottles or cause the silicone to degrade. To maintain the quality and integrity of your bottle, it is best to hand wash it with a gentle soap and warm water using a soft brush or sponge. 


### How can I prevent condensation on the outside of the bottle?

Condensation can occur if the outside temperature is warm, and the water inside the bottle is cold. To minimize condensation, pre-Chill your bottle by filling it with ice or cold water and letting it sit in the refrigerator for a few hours before use. This will help keep the temperature of your drink consistent and reduce any formation of water droplets on the outside. Additionally, using a bottle sleeve or insulated case can also help reduce condensation and keep your hands dry while handling the bottle.
