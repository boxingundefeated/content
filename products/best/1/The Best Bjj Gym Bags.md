* Wash frequently: Even if you're not using your bag daily, wash it regularly to avoid build-up of dirt and bacteria. Check the care instructions provided by the manufacturer for proper cleaning methods.

* Use odor-eliminating products: Spritz the inside of your bag with odor-eliminating spray or add a small piece of fabric softener sheet to neutralize smells.
