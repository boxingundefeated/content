3. Storage Care: When not in use, store your water bottle in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prolong its lifespan.

### What are Built Water Bottles?

Built Water Bottles are durable, high-quality water bottles designed for active lifestyles and outdoor enthusiasts. They are made from strong, lightweight materials and often feature leak-proof caps and easy-grip designs. These bottles are ideal for carrying water, sports drinks, or any other beverage on-the-go. 


### Which materials are used in making Built Water Bottles?

Built Water Bottles are generally made using materials like stainless steel, Tritan plastic, or glass. Stainless steel bottles are known for their strength and insulating properties, while Tritan plastic offers lightweight and shatter-resistant benefits. Glass bottles are suitable for those who prefer non-toxic and eco-friendly options.

### How do Built Water Bottles keep water cold or hot?

Built Water Bottles often come with double-walled insulation, which helps to maintain the temperature of the beverage inside. This vacuum-insulated design prevents heat transfer, keeping your water colder for longer periods or maintaining your hot drinks at a consistent temperature. 


### Can Built Water Bottles be used for both hot and cold beverages?

Yes, most Built Water Bottles are designed to handle both hot and cold beverages. However, some bottles may not be suitable for extreme temperature variations, so it is essential to check the manufacturer's specifications before purchasing.

### How do I clean my Built Water Bottle?

Cleaning your Built Water Bottle depends on the material it's made of. For stainless steel bottles, you can wash them with mild soap and water, using a bottle brush to reach the bottom. Tritan plastic or glass bottles can be cleaned similarly, but some may be dishwasher-safe for added convenience. Always refer to the manufacturer's instructions for proper care and maintenance of your water bottle. 


### How do I know if a Built Water Bottle is leak-proof?
