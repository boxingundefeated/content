### Which materials are best for clear water bottles?

Both glass and BPA-free plastic are excellent choices for clear water bottles. Glass is more eco-friendly and inert, which prevents leaching of chemicals, but it is also heavier. BPA-free plastic is lightweight and more resistant to breakage; however, some users may prefer glass due to potential concerns over the long-term safety of plastics. 


### How do I clean my clear water bottle?

Daily cleanings should begin by emptying out the bottle and thoroughly rinsing it with water. Then, use a gentle dish soap and a non-abrasive sponge or brush to clean the inside and outside surfaces. For persistent odors or stains, you can also use a mixture of equal parts water and vinegar and let it sit in the bottle for several hours before rinsing thoroughly.

### How can I maintain and prolong the life of my clear water bottle?

* Wash your water bottle daily.

* Avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures, which might cause cracking or melting.

* Store the water bottle upright and open the cap to promote air circulation and prevent bacteria growth.

* Avoid adding hot beverages, as they may warp or melt the plastic, or cause the glass to crack.
