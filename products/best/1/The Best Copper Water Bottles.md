## FAQ


### Why are copper water bottles good for you?

Copper water bottles are considered good for your health as they help in the ion exchange process that makes water more alkaline. This alkaline water can aid in detoxifying the body, boosting immunity, and improving digestion.

### How long should water sit in a copper bottle?

It is recommended to let water sit in a copper bottle for at least 6-8 hours for the ion exchange process to occur. Some people prefer to keep water overnight, for about 8-10 hours. The longer you leave the water in the bottle, the more alkaline it becomes. 


### Do copper water bottles need to be cleaned regularly?

Yes, copper water bottles need to be cleaned regularly to prevent the buildup of harmful bacteria and maintain the quality of the copper. It is advised to clean your bottle at least once a week with a mixture of water, lemon juice, and baking soda.

### Can you put hot water in a copper water bottle?

No, it is not recommended to put hot water in a copper water bottle as it can cause the copper to corrode and negatively affect the taste of the water. Use lukewarm or room temperature water instead. 


### How long do copper water bottles last?

With proper care and maintenance, a high-quality copper water bottle can last for several years. It is essential to clean and dry the bottle regularly to prevent the formation of a green patina or deposits inside the bottle. 


### Are there any health concerns about using copper water bottles?

While copper is beneficial for your health, using a copper water bottle that is damaged or has a buildup of bacteria can lead to health concerns. Always inspect your bottle for any damage, and ensure you clean it regularly and properly to maintain its quality and safety. 


### How do I know if a copper water bottle is authentic?

An authentic copper water bottle will have a natural reddish-brown color and will develop a greenish patina over time. Look for a smooth, polished surface and avoid any bottles with a rough or uneven finish. Additionally, read reviews and consider purchasing from reputable sellers to ensure you are getting a genuine product.
