### How does double insulation work?

Double insulation works by creating a vacuum between two layers of stainless steel or other insulating materials. This vacuum prevents heat from passing through, while the outer layer reflects heat away from the bottle. As a result, the temperature of the liquid inside remains stable for longer periods.
