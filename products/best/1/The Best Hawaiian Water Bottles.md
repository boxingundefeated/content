### Which is better, a stainless steel or glass Hawaiian water bottle?

Both stainless steel and glass Hawaiian water bottles have their own advantages and disadvantages. Stainless steel is more durable, able to withstand impacts and retain temperatures for a longer time, but may produce a metallic taste for some users. Glass, on the other hand, is more transparent, easy to clean, and provides a pure taste, but is more fragile and may not retain temperatures as well as steel. 

* Stainless steel: durability, retaining temperature, metallic taste for some users

* Glass: transparency, easy to clean, pure taste, more fragile, may not retain temperatures as well


### How can I clean my Hawaiian water bottle?

Rinse the bottle thoroughly and let it air dry. Glass bottles can be cleaned using the same method or by placing them in the dishwasher, if they are dishwasher-safe. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals on your water bottle to prevent damaging the design or surface.
