### How heavy should my kettlebell be?

The ideal weight depends on your fitness level and experience with weight training. Most beginners can start with an 8-15 kg (18-33 lbs) kettlebell, while more experienced trainers can use a heavier weight, about 15-30 kg (33-66 lbs). 


### How often should I use kettlebells for golf training?

Consistent training is essential for improvement. Aim to incorporate kettlebell exercises into your golf training routine at least 2-3 times per week, allowing for adequate rest and recovery. 


### How can I incorporate kettlebells into my golf practice sessions?

* Warm up before exercising to prevent injury.

* Use proper form and technique while lifting kettlebells.

* Allow sufficient time for recovery and rest between training sessions.
