## Buyer's Guide


### Important Features to Consider

When choosing a life water bottle, there are several key features that you should pay attention to: 

* Capacity: Determine how much liquid you need to carry with you. Common sizes include 24 ounces, 32 ounces, or more.

* Insulation: Consider whether you want a bottle that keeps your water cold or hot, and choose one with proper insulation.
