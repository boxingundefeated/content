### Important Features to Consider

When shopping for Lululemon yoga mats, there are several key features to keep in mind: 

* Thickness: The thickness of a yoga mat can greatly affect your comfort during practice. Lululemon offers mats in various thicknesses, usually ranging from 3 to 5mm. Thinner mats provide a better grip on the ground, while thicker ones offer more cushioning.

* Material: Lululemon yoga mats are made from high-quality materials like natural rubber, which provide excellent grip and durability. Some mats may contain polyurethane or other synthetic materials as well.

* Size: Consider the dimensions of the mat, as Lululemon offers different sizes to accommodate various body types and practice styles.


### Considerations for Yoga Practice Style

Your choice of Lululemon yoga mat will also depend on your preferred yoga practice style: 

1. Vinyasa or Ashtanga: If you practice dynamic styles like Vinyasa or Ashtanga, look for a non-slip mat with good traction to keep you stable during your flow.

2. Restorative or Yin Yoga: For slower-paced practices that emphasize relaxation and recovery, choose a thicker mat with more cushioning to support joints and maintain comfort during longer holds.

3. Hot Yoga or Bikram: If you practice in a heated environment, opt for a mat with moisture-wicking properties to keep you dry and prevent slipping during your session.
