### What is a recumbent bike?

A recumbent bike is a type of exercise bike that features a reclined seat with back support and the foot pedals placed forward. This design allows for a more comfortable workout and reduces strain on the lower back and joints compared to traditional upright bikes. 


### How does the Nautilus recumbent bike work?

The Nautilus recumbent bike functions by providing resistance to the pedals as you cycle. This resistance can be adjusted to different levels to increase or decrease the intensity of your workout. The reclined seat and foot pedals position allow for a more relaxed posture while exercising, making it a popular choice among people with joint pain or lower back issues.
