When it comes to choosing an Osprey gym bag, there are several important features to consider:

Before making your final decision, consider the following factors:

* **Style and Personal Preferences:**  Although functionality is key, you may also want to consider the appearance and style of the bag. There are a variety of colors, designs, and aesthetics to choose from in the Osprey gym bag line, so find one that fits your personal taste and preferences.

### General Advice for Maintaining Your Osprey Gym Bag

To ensure your Osprey gym bag stays in optimal condition for years to come, follow these simple maintenance tips: 

* **Clean Your Bag Regularly:**  Wipe down the exterior with a damp cloth and mild detergent. For the interior, use a soft brush or vacuum to remove debris and dust. Occasionally, deep clean the bag by following the manufacturer's instructions.

* **Air It Out:**  After every workout, air out your gym bag to prevent odors from building up. Open all zippers and compartments, and leave it in a well-ventilated area to dry completely.

* **Store Your Bag Properly:**  To maintain its shape and structure, store your gym bag in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. If possible, place it in a well-ventilated area or use a breathable storage solution.
