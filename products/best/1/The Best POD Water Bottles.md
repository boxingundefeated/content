### What is a POD water bottle?

A POD water bottle is a reusable, refillable water bottle with interchangeable parts, including a bottom water reservoir (POD), a middle filter, and a top cap with a mouthpiece. These bottles allow users to easily refill and filter their water on-the-go without compromising on portability or convenience. 


### How do POD water bottles work?

POD water bottles work by filtering and purifying the water you drink. When you fill the bottle with water, it passes through the middle filter, which removes impurities and contaminants such as bacteria, viruses, and chemicals. The filtered water then collects in the bottom reservoir (POD), ready for you to drink through the top cap and mouthpiece.

### How many refills does a POD water bottle filter last?

The lifespan of a POD water bottle filter varies depending on the quality of the water you filter, how often you use the bottle, and the specific filter you choose. Most POD water bottle filters have a capacity of filtering approximately 40 gallons (151 L) of water before needing to be replaced. However, always check the manufacturer's recommendations for your specific filter model. 


### What type of water can I use in a POD water bottle?

You can use a variety of water sources with a POD water bottle, including tap water, well water, and even untreated water from a stream or lake. The advanced filter technology in POD water bottles is designed to remove bacteria, viruses, and chemicals from most water sources, ensuring that you have clean, safe drinking water wherever you are. 


### How do I clean and maintain my POD water bottle?

Cleaning and maintaining your POD water bottle is important to ensure optimal performance and longevity. To clean your bottle, follow these steps: 

1. Disassemble the bottle by removing the POD, filter, and cap

2. Wash all parts with warm, soapy water

3. Rinse thoroughly with clean water

4. Air dry all parts separately

5. Reassemble the bottle and store it in a clean, dry location

You should also replace the filter and POD as recommended by the manufacturer to maintain clean, safe drinking water. 


### Can I freeze my POD water bottle?

Technically, yes, you can freeze your POD water bottle. However, it is not recommended, as the expansion of water as it freezes may potentially damage the bottle, filter, or cap. Additionally, freezing may alter the filter's performance, possibly reducing its effectiveness at removing contaminants from the water. It is best to store your POD water bottle in a cool, dry location when not in use.
