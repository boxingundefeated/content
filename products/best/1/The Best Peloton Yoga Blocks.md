## Buyer's Guide

Peloton yoga blocks are essential tools for deepening your practice and making poses more accessible. Below are some important features, considerations, and advice when choosing the right yoga blocks for you. 


### Size and Shape

Yoga blocks are available in various sizes and shapes. Standard sizes include 3x6x9 inches (smaller) and 4x6x9 inches (larger). Select the size that suits your body type and comfort preferences. Shape varieties include rectangular and cylindrical blocks. Rectangular ones provide a stable, flat surface, while cylindrical ones can be used as rolls for deeper stretches.

### Materials

Yoga blocks are typically made from foam, cork, or wood. Foam tends to be lightweight and affordable, but can degrade over time. Cork is heavier, provides better grip, and is eco-friendly. Wooden blocks are more durable and firm, offering stability during asanas. Choose the material that aligns with your preferences and needs. 


### Texture and Grip

Ensure your yoga block has a non-slip texture, especially if you practice in an environment with high humidity. Some foam blocks come with a rubberized grip for added stability. Consider the texture of the material when making your choice, since it directly impacts your grip during practice. 


### Durability and Longevity

Your yoga block should be able to withstand daily use and maintain its shape and integrity over time. Cork and wood blocks offer greater durability and are less likely to develop dents or cracks compared to foam. If you choose a foam block, opt for higher density foam, as it will last longer.

### Consider Your Yoga Practice Style

Choose a yoga block that best accommodates your practice style and needs. If you primarily focus on restorative or gentle yoga, a larger, softer foam block may suit you better. For more dynamic styles like YogaVibes, Vinyasa, or Ashtanga, a harder, denser cork or wood block will likely be more appropriate. 


### Compatibility with Existing Equipment

Ensure your chosen yoga block fits comfortably on your yoga mat and aligns with your other yoga accessories, such as straps or blankets. This will promote ease and efficiency during your practice sessions. 

By considering these features, factors, and recommendations in your decision-making process, you'll be able to select the perfect yoga block to enhance your Peloton yoga practice.
