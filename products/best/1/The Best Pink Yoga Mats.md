### How thick should my pink yoga mat be?

The ideal thickness for a pink yoga mat depends on your personal preferences and needs. Thinner mats (around 2-3mm) are more lightweight and portable, while thicker mats (around 5-10mm) offer more cushioning and support for your joints during practice. 


### Can I use a pink yoga mat for hot yoga?

Yes, you can use a pink yoga mat for hot yoga as long as it is made from a moisture-resistant material like PVC or TPE. However, natural rubber mats may become slippery when wet, so consider using a yoga towel on top of the mat for added grip.
