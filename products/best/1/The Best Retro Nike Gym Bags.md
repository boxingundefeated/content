* Straps and Handles: Choose a bag with comfortable, padded straps or handles that distribute weight evenly to prevent strain on your shoulders or back.

* Branding and Design: Look for a bag that showcases Nike's iconic logo and retro design elements that embody the brand's heritage and history.

### How do I clean my Retro Nike Gym Bag?

To clean your Retro Nike Gym Bag, spot clean any stains with a mild detergent and damp cloth. For overall cleaning, consider using a gentle fabric cleaner and soft brush. Avoid machine washing or dry cleaning, as this can damage the bag's fabric and integrity. 


### How do I store my Retro Nike Gym Bag when not in use?

1. Store the bag in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat.

2. Avoid storing the bag in a damp or humid environment, as it can lead to mold or mildew growth.

3. Make sure to empty the bag and allow it to air out between uses, especially after exposure to moisture or odor-causing materials.
