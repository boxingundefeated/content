### General Advice for Using TAL Water Bottles

To make the most of your TAL water bottle and extend its lifespan, consider the following tips: 

* Cleanliness: Regularly clean your water bottle to prevent the buildup of bacteria or mold. Use warm, soapy water or invest in a bottle brush to reach all areas of the bottle and ensure a thorough clean.

* Storage: Store your water bottle in a cool, dry place when not in use. This helps prevent damage to the bottle and the growth of any unwanted organisms.

* Longevity: To maximize the lifespan of your TAL water bottle, avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures, such as direct sunlight or freezing conditions. Additionally, avoid dropping the bottle or subjecting it to other forms of impact.
