### What is Tritan plastic?

Tritan is a BPA-free, transparent polymer used in the manufacture of water bottles. It has a high resistance to breaking, making it a tough and durable material for water bottles. 


### How does the BPA-free feature of Tritan water bottles protect me?

Bisphenol A (BPA) is an industrial chemical used in the production of certain plastics and resins that can be harmful if ingested. Tritan plastic water bottles are BPA-free, so they are safe for long-term use and do not harm your health when you consume liquids from them. 


### Are Tritan water bottles safe to use?

Yes, Tritan water bottles are safe to use. They are made from high-quality, BPA-free plastic that doesn't leach harmful chemicals into your drinking water. Moreover, Tritan is heat-resistant and recyclable, making it an environmentally friendly choice.

The lifespan of a Tritan water bottle may vary depending on usage and proper care. Generally, Tritan water bottles are designed to last for several years. To extend their lifespan, it's essential to clean them regularly and handle them with care to prevent any damage or scratches to the surface.
