### What are vacuum water bottles?

Vacuum water bottles are insulated water bottles that keep cold beverages chilled and hot beverages warm. They're designed with a double-walled construction, which provides better insulation than single-walled bottles and helps maintain the temperature of your drink for longer periods. 


### How do vacuum water bottles work?

Vacuum water bottles have a unique double-walled structure. The space between the two layers of glass or stainless steel is vacuum-sealed, creating a void that reduces heat transfer. This means your drink stays its intended temperature without using any power or electricity.
