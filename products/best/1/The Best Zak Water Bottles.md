### What types of Zak water bottles are available

Zak produces a variety of water bottles including stainless steel, plastic, glass, and vacuum insulated bottles. Each type has its unique benefits and features. For example, the stainless steel bottles are ideal for those looking for durability and insulation, while the glass bottles are best for those concerned about the taste and quality of their water. 


### How do I clean my Zak water bottle

Cleaning method varies depending on the material of your Zak water bottle. For plastic bottles, you should wash them with warm soapy water. For stainless steel bottles, you can either hand wash with warm soapy water or put them in the dishwasher on the top rack. For glass bottles, they can be cleaned in the dishwasher or by hand. 


### How long does the insulation last on Zak's vacuum insulated water bottles?

Zak's vacuum insulated water bottles are designed to keep your beverages hot or cold for a long time. Precise times vary depending on factors such as the temperature of the beverage initially, but in general, hot drinks can stay warm for up to 12 hours and cold drinks can stay cold for up to 24 hours. 


### How do I replace the cap on my Zak water bottle?

If the cap on your Zak water bottle gets lost or damaged, you can purchase a replacement cap. These are usually available on the Zak website or other online retailers. To replace the cap, simply unscrew the old one, clean the thread, and screw the new one on. 


### Do Zak water bottles contain BPA?

Zak's plastic water bottles are BPA-free. This means they don't contain bisphenol A, a chemical that has been linked to health issues. If you're concerned about BPA, you can choose other types of bottles such as stainless steel or glass that are naturally BPA-free.
