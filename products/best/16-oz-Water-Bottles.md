* Insulation - Helps maintain your drink's temperature (hot or cold) longer.

* Leak-Proof Lid - Prevents unwanted spills and accidents.

* Easy-Grip Design - Makes it comfortable and convenient to carry or hold.


### Considerations

Factor these considerations into your decision-making process: 

* Material - Bottles can be made from stainless steel, glass, or BPA-free plastic.

* Size and Weight - Choose a bottle that's easy to carry and doesn't take up too much space.

* Ease of Cleaning - Look for bottles with wide-mouth openings or removable parts for simple cleaning.
