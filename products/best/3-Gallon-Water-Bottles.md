## FAQ


### How much water does a 3-gallon water bottle hold?

A 3-gallon water bottle holds 11.35 liters of water. This capacity is suitable for sharing among a family or a group of people at work, school, or during outdoor activities. 


### What are the dimensions of a 3-gallon water bottle?

<div><a href="https://serp.ly/@boxingundefeated/amazon/3-gallon-water-bottles?utm_source=boxingundefeated&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=cfpages"><img src="https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/3+Gallon+Water+Bottles-4/w=720,h=540,fit=pad,background=black" alt="3 Gallon Water Bottles-4"></a></div>

The dimensions of a 3-gallon water bottle vary depending on the manufacturer and design. Generally, they have a height of around 27-28 inches (68-71 cm) and a diameter of 10-11 inches (25-28 cm). 


### Do 3-gallon water bottles come with a spigot?

Many 3-gallon water bottles are designed with a built-in spigot for easy dispensing. This feature allows users to pour water directly from the bottle without the need for a cup or glass. Some bottles may require purchasing a separate spigot, so it's essential to check the product's specifications before purchasing. 


### How long does a 3-gallon water bottle last?
