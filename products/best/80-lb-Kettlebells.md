### What material is an 80 lb kettlebell made of?

80 lb kettlebells are typically made from cast iron or heavy-duty steel, ensuring durability and stability during use. Some higher-end models may also feature rubber or vinyl coatings to protect floors and equipment from scratching or chipping. 


### How do I properly grip a kettlebell?

To properly grip an 80 lb kettlebell, place your fingers through the handle's opening and grip it firmly with an overhand grip. Ensure a secure hold and maintain proper alignment throughout each exercise to prevent injury and maximize effectiveness.
