### Why should I buy an 80 oz water bottle?

An 80 oz water bottle is ideal for anyone who wants to stay hydrated throughout the day without needing frequent refills. It's perfect for long workouts, hikes, or any outdoor activity where constant access to water is essential. 


### What materials are common for 80 oz water bottles?

* Stainless steel: Durable, maintains temperature of the beverage, and has no BPA.

* BPA-free plastic: Lightweight and affordable, but may not have temperature retention properties.

* Glass: Maintains taste quality and is BPA-free, but can be heavy and less durable.

### How do I clean my 80 oz water bottle?

To clean your water bottle, use warm soapy water and a gentle bottle brush to reach the bottom and sides. Rinse thoroughly and let air dry or dry with a clean microfiber cloth. For a more thorough cleaning, use a mixture of white vinegar and water to remove any lingering odors or bacteria.

### Can I put hot beverages in an 80 oz water bottle?

It depends on the type of water bottle. Insulated stainless steel water bottles are designed to handle both hot and cold beverages, while plastic and glass bottles are typically not suitable for hot liquids due to the risk of melting or cracking. 


### Do I need a water filter with an 80 oz water bottle?

It depends on where you will be filling your bottle. If you are confident in the quality of the tap water, a water filter may not be necessary. However, if you are concerned about contaminants or taste, a water filter attachment can be a valuable addition to your water bottle. 


### How do I know if I'm drinking enough water throughout the day?

A general guideline is to drink 8 oz of water 8 times a day for a total of about 64 oz. However, individual needs may vary depending on factors such as activity level, climate, and health conditions.
