## FAQ


### What is an Air Water Bottle?

An Air Water Bottle is a reusable water bottle designed to keep your beverages colder or hotter for extended periods by using vacuum insulation. It's an eco-friendly and healthy alternative to single-use plastic bottles. 


### How do Air Water Bottles work?

Air Water Bottles employ vacuum insulation technology, which creates a space between two layers of material where air has been removed. This vacuum space prevents heat from transferring between the inside and outside of the bottle, keeping your drinks either hot or cold as desired.

### Are Air Water Bottles safe to use?

Yes, Air Water Bottles are generally safe to use. Most of them are made from food-grade stainless steel or other safe materials, which do not leach chemicals into your drinks. Always check product specifications and safety certifications before purchasing. 


### How long do Air Water Bottles keep drinks hot or cold?

The insulation efficiency can vary between different models of Air Water Bottles, but many can keep drinks hot for up to 12 hours and cold for up to 24 hours. Check individual product specifications for detailed performance information. 


### Can Air Water Bottles be used with carbonated beverages?

Many Air Water Bottles are designed to handle carbonated drinks, but it's crucial to always follow the manufacturer's recommendations. Some models may not be suitable for carbonated beverages, so check product specifications before using.

### How do I clean my Air Water Bottle?

Cleaning instructions may vary depending on the specific model, but generally, Air Water Bottles can be cleaned with warm soapy water and a non-abrasive sponge or bottle brush. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that could damage the bottle's interior surface. Always rinse thoroughly and let air dry before using again. 


### What is the difference between double-walled and triple-walled Air Water Bottles?

Double-walled bottles have two layers of material (usually stainless steel) with a vacuum space between them. Triple-walled bottles have an additional layer of material, which can further enhance their insulation capabilities. Triple-walled bottles tend to be heavier and more expensive than double-walled bottles.
