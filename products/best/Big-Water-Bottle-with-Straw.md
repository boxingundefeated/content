### What are the benefits of using a water bottle with a straw?

A water bottle with a straw offers several benefits: first, it provides hands-free and mess-free drinking, making it convenient during workouts or outdoor activities. Second, straws minimize contact between your mouth and the bottle, reducing the risk of germ transmission. ### How do I clean a water bottle with a straw?

Cleaning a water bottle with a straw is essential to maintain hygiene and prolong the bottle's lifespan. To clean, follow these steps: 

1. Disassemble the straw from the bottle

2. Rinse the bottle and straw with warm, soapy water

3. Use a small brush or dish sponge to clean the straw thoroughly, ensuring that all debris is removed

4. Rinse the bottle and straw with clean water

5. Air-dry the bottle and straw before reassembling and using again
