## Buyer's Guide

When choosing an hourly water bottle, you want to ensure that it's designed for easy hydration throughout your day. Here are some features, considerations, and advice to help you make the right choice: 


### Features

* Volume: Hourly water bottles usually have a volume of 16-32 oz (473-946 ml) to provide enough water for multiple refills throughout your day.

* Time Markers: Look for a bottle with hourly time markers on the side, which will help you keep track of your water intake for each hour.

* Lid Design: Choose a water bottle with a leak-proof lid and a removable cap for easy refills and cleaning.

* Durability: Find an hourly water bottle made from high-quality materials that can withstand daily use and keep your water clean and odor-free.

### Considerations

* Bottle Shape: Consider the shape and size of the water bottle to ensure it fits easily in your bag, car cupholder, or at your workstation.

* Stability: Opt for a water bottle with a flat bottom or a wide base for added stability when standing on a desk or table.

* Ease of Use: Choose a bottle with a wide mouth for easy filling and cleaning, and a soft-touch spout for comfortable drinking.


### General Advice

* Cleanliness: Regularly wash your hourly water bottle with warm, soapy water and let it air dry to maintain its cleanliness and hygiene.

* Customization: Purchase a bottle with a removable label or choose a clear water bottle, so you can easily add stickers or write your name on it for personalization.

* Carry Extra: Always keep an additional bottle or refill your current one during long days or extended periods away from home, as staying hydrated is essential for your health and well-being.
