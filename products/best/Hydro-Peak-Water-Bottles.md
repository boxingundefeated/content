### Key Features of Hydro Peak Water Bottles

There are several features you should consider when buying a hydro peak water bottle: 

* Capacity: Hydro peak water bottles come in different sizes, typically between 16 oz (0.5 L) and 64 oz (2 L). Choose the capacity that suits your needs best. If you'll be using it for workouts, opt for a smaller size to save on weight and space. For long hikes, consider a larger size for longer periods of hydration.

* Material: Water bottles are made from various materials, including plastic (BPA-free), stainless steel, glass, and silicone. Each has its advantages and disadvantages.
