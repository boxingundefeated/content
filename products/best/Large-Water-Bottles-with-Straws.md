### 6. How do I clean a large water bottle with a straw?

Regular cleaning is essential to maintain the integrity and hygiene of your large water bottle with a straw. Use warm, soapy water and a bottle brush to thoroughly clean the interior and a damp cloth to wipe down the exterior. For the straw itself, use a pipe cleaner or brush specifically designed for cleaning straws, ensuring that you clean both the interior and exterior surfaces. Rinse the bottle thoroughly and allow it to air dry. For a deeper clean, consider using a mixture of equal parts warm water and vinegar to disinfect the bottle and straw, followed by a thorough rinse.
