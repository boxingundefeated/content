### Important Features

When looking for large yoga blocks, consider the following features: 

* Size: Choose a block that is large enough to provide ample support and comfort during your practice.

* Material: Quality materials like foam or cork can offer durability and support.

* Non-slip Surface: A non-slip surface will keep you stable and secure during your practice.

* Ease of Cleaning: Look for a block that can be easily wiped clean or is machine washable.


### Considerations

Before purchasing large yoga blocks, consider the following: 

* Intended Use: Consider whether you need the extra height and support provided by large yoga blocks or if smaller blocks would suffice for your practice.
