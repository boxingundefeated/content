### How do I clean a Light Up Water Bottle?

To clean a Light Up Water Bottle, follow these steps: 

1. Empty the bottle and dispose of the water responsibly.

2. Remove any removable parts, such as the cap or straw, and wash them using warm, soapy water and a bottle brush.

3. Rinse the bottle thoroughly, ensuring that all soap residue is removed.

4. Air dry the bottle and its parts before reassembling and using it again.


### Are these water bottles dishwasher-safe?

Light Up Water Bottles may have electronic components, such as the LED light and battery compartment, that are not dishwasher-safe. It is recommended to hand wash these items to prevent potential damage. 


### How do I replace the batteries in my Light Up Water Bottle?

To replace the batteries in your water bottle: 

1. Unscrew the cap or bottom of the bottle to access the battery compartment.

2. Remove the old batteries and recycle them responsibly.

3. Insert the new batteries, ensuring that they are installed correctly (usually with the positive end facing up).

4. Replace the cap or bottom of the bottle and test the light to ensure proper functionality.
