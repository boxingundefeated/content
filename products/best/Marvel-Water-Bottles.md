### Do these bottles keep beverages hot or cold?

Yes! Many Marvel Water Bottles feature double-walled vacuum insulation that helps maintain the temperature of your beverage. Some bottles are designed for specific purposes, such as hot drinks or cold drinks, so make sure you choose the right bottle best suited for your preferences. 


### How do I clean my Marvel Water Bottle?

* If your bottle is dishwasher safe, follow the manufacturer's instructions for proper cleaning.

* For plastic or glass bottles that aren't dishwasher safe, use warm soapy water and a bottle brush to reach the bottom. Rinse thoroughly and let it air dry upside down.

* For stainless steel bottles, use a small brush to clean the inside thoroughly. Rinse with warm soapy water, then rinse with clean water. Dry with a soft cloth or let air dry upside down.
