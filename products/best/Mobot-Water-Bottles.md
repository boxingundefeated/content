* Material: Mobot water bottles are often made from food-grade stainless steel or borosilicate glass, which are both non-toxic and safe for long-term use.

* Lid and Spout Design: Different bottle designs offer varying levels of convenience in use. Choose one that suits your drinking preferences, whether it's a twist-off cap, flip-top spout, or straw top.


### Considerations

Before purchasing a Mobot water bottle, think about these factors: 

1. Longevity: Durable bottles will withstand the wear and tear of daily use and minimize spills and leaks. Choose a bottle with high-quality materials, such as stainless steel with a powder coat finish to prevent scratches and dings.

2. Cleaning and Maintenance: Some bottles are dishwasher-safe, while others require manual cleaning. Look for models with wide mouths or included cleaning tools to simplify the process.

3. Portability and Compactness: If you're looking for a water bottle to take on hikes, runs, or to the gym, prioritize lightweight and easy-to-carry bottles with leak-proof caps and attachment points for carabiners or lanyards.


### General Advice

Here are a few tips to help you make the best water bottle choice: 

* Test the Lid: Look for a leak-proof seal and flip-top lid that stays firmly in place but is easy to operate with one hand.

* Assess the Mouth: Large mouth openings are ideal for filling and adding ice, while narrow spouts are better for controlled drinking.

* Check the Straw Functionality: If you prefer drinking from a straw, confirm that it's comfortable to use and can be easily cleaned.
