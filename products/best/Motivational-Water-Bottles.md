### Features to Look For in Motivational Water Bottles

Motivational water bottles are designed not only to keep you hydrated but also to encourage you to drink more water through inspiring quotes, affirmations, or pictures. Key features you should consider when choosing a motivational water bottle include: 

* Size and capacity: Pick a bottle that's suitable for your daily water intake and on-the-go needs.

* Material: Look for bottles made of high-quality, safe materials like BPA-free plastic, stainless steel, or glass.

* Motivational aspect: Choose a bottle with messages or designs that resonate with you and help keep you motivated.


### Important Considerations

Before making your decision, consider the following: 

* Functionality: Make sure the bottle is leak-proof, easy to carry, and has a comfortable grip.

* Ease of use: Opt for a bottle with a wide mouth for easy filling and a straw or spout for easy drinking.

* Cleaning and maintenance: Choose a bottle that's dishwasher safe or easy to clean by hand.
