### 1. Durability and Material

Look for gym bags made from high-quality materials that are designed to withstand regular use and wear. Durable materials like nylon, polyester, or leather are ideal. Also, consider water-resistant options to keep your belongings dry in case of rain or accidental spills.

### 3. Comfort and Carrying Options

Select a gym bag with comfortable shoulder straps, padded handles, or a combination of both for optimal carrying comfort. Some models also feature adjustable straps to ensure a customized fit. 


### 4. Ventilation and Airflow

To prevent odors and mildew, look for gym bags with mesh or ventilated compartments to increase airflow, especially for wet or damp items like shoes and sweaty clothes. 


### 5. Shoe Compartment and Separate Comartments

A designated shoe compartment is an essential feature for keeping your shoes separate from other gym gear and minimizing odor transfer. Additionally, multiple compartments make it easier to organize and locate your belongings quickly.
