### How do I clean a pink water bottle with a straw?

Cleaning a pink water bottle with a straw can be done easily in a few steps: 

* Remove the straw and lid from the bottle and wash them separately with warm soapy water or place them in the dishwasher for a thorough clean.

* Fill the bottle with warm water and a few drops of dish soap, replace the lid and shake it vigorously to clean the interior of the bottle thoroughly. Alternatively, use a clean bottle brush to reach the bottom and corners of the bottle.

* Rinse the bottle well with water to remove any soap residue. For a more thorough cleaning, you can also use white vinegar to disinfect the bottle or use specialized cleaning tablets available specifically for water bottles.
