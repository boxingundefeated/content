Caring for and cleaning your plastic-free water bottle is essential to maintain its performance and extend its lifespan. Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for cleaning and maintenance. ### How much do plastic-free water bottles cost compared to traditional plastic bottles?

Plastic-free water bottles may cost slightly more than their traditional plastic counterparts, but their durability and long-lasting nature often offset the upfront expense. ### Will using a plastic-free water bottle affect the taste of my water?

No, using a plastic-free water bottle will not alter the taste of your water. Unlike plastic bottles, which can release chemicals and odors into the water, eco-friendly materials such as glass, stainless steel, and silicone do not affect the taste, odor, or quality of the water they hold.
