## FAQ


### How do recumbent bikes differ from traditional bicycles?

Recumbent bikes have a reclined seat and a lower center of gravity, providing more back support and comfort during exercise. In comparison, traditional bicycles typically have an upright seat, which can put more strain on the back and cause discomfort for some users. 


### What are the benefits of using a recumbent bike?

Recumbent bikes offer low-impact exercise that is easy on the joints, making them suitable for people with joint issues or those recovering from injuries. Additionally, they provide a comfortable workout experience, allowing users to exercise for longer periods and burn more calories.
