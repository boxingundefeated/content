Scented water bottles are more than just a tool to carry your water. They have unique features that make them stand out from standard water bottles. Here are some important features you should consider before making your purchase: 

1. Scent Infusion System: Look for a bottle with a good scent infusion system that mixes the scent properly and lasts a long time.

2. Capacity: Consider the capacity of the bottle. It should be sufficient for your daily water intake needs.

3. Material: The material of the bottle is also important. Opt for BPA-free materials like stainless steel or glass.

4. Ease of Cleaning: The bottle should be easy to clean. Look for designs with wide mouths or removable infusion baskets for easier cleaning.


### Types of Scented Water Bottles

Scented water bottles come in various shapes and sizes. Here are the main types: 

* Straw Bottles: These bottles have a straw that allows you to drink without tipping the bottle.

* Squeeze Bottles: These are designed for active use. They're easy to squeeze and can help you increase your water intake.

* Infuser Bottles: These have an infuser basket where you can put your scented water. The infusion basket helps distribute the scent evenly.

* Regular Tumblers: These are simple water bottles with no additional features except for the scent infusion system.


### General Advice

Here are some general pieces of advice when using scented water bottles: 

1. Change the water and scent regularly to maintain freshness and effective scent diffusion.

2. Clean your bottle thoroughly after every use to prevent bacteria buildup.

3. Choose a scent that you enjoy and that doesn't overpower other flavors if you're also using the bottle for regular water.

4. Experiment with different scents to find the one that suits your taste and mood best.
