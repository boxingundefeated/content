## FAQ


### What are Smart Kettlebells?

Smart Kettlebells are advanced fitness devices that combine the traditional kettlebell workout with digital tracking and monitoring features. They often come with accompanying mobile apps that allow users to set goals, track progress, and access workout tutorials. 


### How do Smart Kettlebells differ from regular kettlebells?

Regular kettlebells are simple, cast-iron weights used for various strength and conditioning exercises. Smart Kettlebells, on the other hand, incorporate technology such as sensors to measure workout information and connect to mobile apps for enhanced user experience. Some models also offer adjustable weight settings for versatility.
