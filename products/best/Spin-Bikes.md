* Effective cardiovascular workout that strengthens heart and lungs

* Builds lean muscle in the lower body and core

* Improves joint flexibility and mobility

* Burns calories efficiently, promoting weight loss

* Offers a low-impact exercise option that is easy on the joints

### How is a spin bike different from a regular stationary bike?

The main difference between a spin bike and a regular stationary bike is the flywheel. Spin bikes have a heavy flywheel that provides resistance and a smoother, more natural ride feel.
