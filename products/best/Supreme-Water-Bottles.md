### How do I clean a Supreme Water Bottle?

To clean your Supreme Water Bottle, follow these steps:   

1. Fill the bottle with warm water and a few drops of dish soap. Shake it vigorously to loosen any residue.   

2. Empty the soapy water and rinse the bottle thoroughly with clean water.   

3. For a more thorough clean, use a bottle brush to reach the bottom and sides of the bottle.   

4. Air-dry the bottle upside down or use a clean, dry cloth to wipe it dry. Avoid using rough or abrasive materials that could damage the bottle's surface.
