### Considerations Before Buying

Before making a purchase, consider the following: 

* Lid Design: Check if the lid of the Titan water bottle is easy to open and close securely. A leak-proof lid prevents spills while on-the-go.

* Maintenance: Look for a bottle that is easy to clean, as proper hygiene is crucial for maintaining the quality of your water. Some bottles have wide-mouth openings for easier cleaning, while others have threaded caps or specialized lids designed with cleaning in mind.

* Compatibility: If you use a water filter or other attachments, ensure that your chosen water bottle is compatible with these accessories.

* Brand Reputation: Research the brand to ensure they have a good track record for producing high-quality products and offering reliable customer support.


### General Advice for Care and Maintenance

To keep your Titan water bottle in optimal condition, follow these care and maintenance tips: 

1. Clean your bottle regularly with warm, soapy water. For thorough cleaning, consider using a specialty bottle brush or dish soap designed specifically for water bottles.

2. Allow your bottle to air dry completely before reassembling it. This helps prevent mold and bacteria growth.

3. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or sharp objects that could scratch or damage the surface of your water bottle.
