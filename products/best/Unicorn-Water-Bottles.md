### How do I clean a unicorn water bottle?

Cleaning your unicorn water bottle is essential for maintaining the quality and freshness of your beverages. To clean your bottle, follow these steps: 

1. Disassemble the water bottle by removing any detachable pieces, such as the cap or straw.

2. Create a cleaning solution by mixing warm water with mild dish soap. Alternatively, you can use equal parts white vinegar and water for a more natural solution.
